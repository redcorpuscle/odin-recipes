Green Salad Recipe

Well, let me introduce you to my favorite green salad recipe. It's simple, but it doesn't skimp at all on texture or flavor. I toss in Parmesan cheese for nutty, salty flavor, and tamari roasted almonds for crunch. To bring it all together, I drizzle on my go-to lemon vinaigrette. It makes this salad zingy, bright, and oh-so-versatile. It pairs just as nicely with pasta as it does with a veggie burger, and it tastes great with classic brunch fare too. If you need a last-minute side dish to round out your Easter menu this weekend, this green salad would be perfect.

Ingredients

  • Half of a head of iceberg lettuce
  • 2 slices of white cheese
  • 3 tablespoons pine nuts
  • 2-3 tablespoons olive oil
  • Juice of a half lemon
  • Salt
  • Pepper flakes

Directions

  1. Wash and dry the ice berg and other vegetables
  2. Chop ceberg coarsely and others finely and put in a bowl
  3. Combine the salt, olive oil and lemon juice together and mix well, pour over the vegetables, mix well
  4. Take the pine nuts into a pan and saute on a hot stove until lightly brown
  5. Chop the cheese on the salad
  6. Sprinkle over pine nuts and pepper flakes
